ABOUT THE EVENT

Robo Yugam 2K26 is the ultimate 24-hour robotics hackathon designed to push the boundaries of innovation. We are bringing together the brightest minds—engineers, developers, creators, and visionaries—to build next-generation solutions.

Whether your expertise lies in core Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), or Automation, this is your arena. Build hardware prototypes, code intelligent software, and shape the future.
